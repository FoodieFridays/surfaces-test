Parametric Equation: \[ \begin{align} x &= (R + r \cos \theta)\cos \phi\\ y &= (R + r \cos \theta)\sin \phi\\ z &= r \sin \theta \end{align} \] where \(\theta, \phi \in [0, 2\pi)\), \(R\) is the distance from the center of the tube to the center of the torus, and \(r\) is the radius of the tube.
